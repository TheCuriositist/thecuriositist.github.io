The Curiositist

Heavily influenced by the late 60s rock and modern Rock Alternative and Indie bands, The Curiositist aims to make music that feels both new-modern and old-familiar.

Music and Style

Growing up in the Chicago Suburbs, his music is heavily influenced by blues and classic rock. Most of his modern inspiration comes from Indie Rock Bands.
