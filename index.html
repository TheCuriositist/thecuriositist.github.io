Of Mistakes and Regrets is my first full-length album.

This album reflects a difficult period in my life, following a hard break-up and some big personal struggles.
Each song tells a story, but some of them are interelated. I'll leave it to you to figure out which and in what ways.
I hope these works speak to you in the way they speak to me.

Available for Streaming in early May, everywhere you listen.
