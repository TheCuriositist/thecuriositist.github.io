Of Mistakes and Regrets
May 1st, 2019 || Album

Released in Spring 2019, this album was as much a passion project as it was an exploration of music production. As my first full length album, it holds that place in my acheivements, but it is definitely not my proudest work. Most popular of the tracks is "I Don't Know", a song I wrote back in the summer of 2017 for solo-acoustic. The song I am most proud of from this release is "If Only". It was written in its entirety in about 3 hours, with very few changes.
