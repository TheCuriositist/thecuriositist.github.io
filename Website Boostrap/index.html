You, my five track EP and debut album.

I guess this album was started last year, when I wrote "I Don't Know."
It was towards the end of Spring semester that that song came together. The rest was written in the span of three days in the beginning of December.
While the songs were done, the final recording took place roughly mid-February through the first week of March.

I hope you can enjoy listening to this album as much I enjoyed writing it.
