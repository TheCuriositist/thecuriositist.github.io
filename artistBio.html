Bio

I started playing guitar when I was 9 and ended taking lessons for 10 years.
The first piece of music I wrote was in a blues band class, but I didn't really get into composition until fall of 2016.
My first real experience working with a recording studio program was Protools in Spring of 2018, but I ended finding I liked Studio One better.
Recording made me realize I really needed to work on playing more than just guitar, and that's when I started into piano and electric bass.
It was playing piano that opened me up to the sound that's most prominant on the first four tracks of my new album "Of Mistakes and Regrets".
