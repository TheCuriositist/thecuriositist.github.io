Santa Proactively Cancels Christmas Flight Amid Coronavirus Fears

March 17th, 2020

Amid growing coronovirus fears, Santa has proactively cancelled his
Dec. 25th ride.
"It's just too dangerous. With all the disinfectant wipes I would need to carry to clean off the sleigh after every house, there'd be no space for presents. And as someone who visits billions of people in one night, I would become a terribly dangerous vector for transmission. I hope everyone understands these are the hard decisions we must make in times like these."
